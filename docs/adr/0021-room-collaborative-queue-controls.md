# Room Collaborative Queue Controls

**Pin to Next** and **Skip** remain available to any connected **Paired Slave**, while **Remove Queued Song** remains limited to the **Paired Slave** that owns the target **Queued Song**. This treats queue ordering and playback advancement as room collaboration, while preserving ownership only for destructive removal of someone else's queued song; another **Slave** does not gain removal rights just because the owner disconnects or expires, and orphaned queued songs remain in the **Playback Queue** until normal playback controls move past them. Because **Skip** is an irreversible live playback action, the **Slave** UI should use a local two-click confirmation without introducing **Server**-side pending state, and the **Server** should reject **Skip** when there is no **Current Song**; stale **Master** reports for **Song End** or **Unplayable Song** remain successful no-ops when they do not match a current queue id. **Master** disconnects do not clear, block controls for, or independently advance the queue, aligning playback advancement with ADR-0011's Master-owned playback progress. **Pin to Next** does not need confirmation because it only reorders upcoming songs and is a successful no-op when the target is already next, and **Remove Queued Song** does not need confirmation because it applies only to the user's own unplayed queued songs and can be recovered by adding the song again.
